Restaurants in the Greater Indianapolis Area

Pho VN

Located on the north east side of Indianpolis on 116th st, Pho VN offers authentic Vietnamese food from a wide variety of options. Made from a real Vietnamese chef, there are items such as pho, fried rice, mi xao gion, and many more!

Rating: 4.5/5.0 456 Google Reviews

Pho Tasty

Located in the Castleton area right off of east 82nd street, Pho Tasty has only been open for less than a decade and has been able to establish it's place on a list of best authentic Vietnamese food in Indianapolis! They serve traditional Vietnamese dishes and offer to-go items for desserts such as Che!

Rating: 4.5/5.0 607 Google Reviews

Pho 21

Found on the east side of Indianapolis, as you exit off I-465, Pho 21 will be right in your conveinience to give a try! Although hidden and small, do not be mis led by their location. Offering amazon Banh Xeo and of course, pho, Pho 21 is another fan favorite to give a try with authentic Vietnamese dishes!

Rating: 4.5/5.0 391 Google Reviews

Eggroll Number 1

On the Southside of Indianapolis is Eggroll #1. This spot offers a variety of Vietnamese dishes and is the most popular among the four listed! With dishes like Pho, com tam and goi cuon, Eggroll #1 has it all! With over two thousand google reviews, it would only make sense to give the place a try!

Rating: 4.6/5.0 2,275 Google Reviews
