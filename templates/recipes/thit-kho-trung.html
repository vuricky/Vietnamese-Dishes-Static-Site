Thịt kho trứng Recipe

Main Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs (700g) pork belly or pork shoulder, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar (or caramel sauce if available)
  • 1/2 tsp salt and 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro and sliced chili (optional for extra spice and taste)

Directions

  1. Prepare the pork: Blanch the pork pieces in boiling water for 2-3 minutes to remove impurities. Rinse under cold water and set aside.
  2. Make the caramel base: Heat the oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the sugar and stir constantly until it melts and turns golden brown. Be careful not to burn it. Quickly add the minced garlic and shallots, stirring until fragrant.
  3. Braise the pork: Add the blanched pork pieces to the caramel and stir to coat evenly. Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper, and continue stirring for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add liquid: Pour in the coconut water, ensuring the pork is submerged. If needed, add additional water to cover. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover and cook for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the eggs Add the boiled eggs to the pot and gently stir to coat them with the sauce. Continue to simmer for another 15-20 minutes until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Assemble the bowls: Divide the cooked noodles among serving bowls. Add slices of brisket and raw sirloin on top. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and beef, cooking the raw sirloin in the heat of the broth.
  8. Serve: Serve hot with steamed jasmine rice. Garnish with cilantro and sliced chili, if desired.
