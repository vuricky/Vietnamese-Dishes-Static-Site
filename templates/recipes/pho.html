Ingredients for broth

  • 3 lbs (1.5 kg) beef bones (marrow, knuckle, or oxtail)
  • 1 lb (450g) beef brisket
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 1 piece of ginger (4 inches), halved
  • 5 star anise pods
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 tbsp of fish sauce, 1tbsp of sugar, and salt to taste

Directions

  1. Prepare the bones and brisket: Blanch the beef bones and brisket: Cover them with water in a pot, bring to a boil, and cook for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse the bones and brisket to remove impurities. In a clean pot, add the bones, brisket, and 4 quarts (4 liters) of water. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer.
  2. Char the onion and ginger: Over an open flame or under a broiler, char the onion and ginger until blackened. Add them to the pot.
  3. Toast the spices: Toast the star anise, cinnamon sticks, cloves, coriander seeds, and fennel seeds in a dry pan until fragrant. Place them in a spice bag or cheesecloth and add to the pot.
  4. Simmer the broth: Simmer the broth gently for 4-6 hours, skimming off any foam or fat. After 1.5-2 hours, remove the brisket, let it cool, and slice thinly. Add fish sauce, sugar, and salt to taste in the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  6. Prepare the noodles: Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
  7. Assemble the bowls: Divide the cooked noodles among serving bowls. Add slices of brisket and raw sirloin on top. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and beef, cooking the raw sirloin in the heat of the broth.
  8. Garnish and serve: Garnish with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, green onions, and sliced chilies. Serve with lime wedges, hoisin sauce, and Sriracha on the side.
