Bánh Mì Full Recipe

Main Ingredients

  • One (or many) baguette of your choice - the thnner, the better as it is common to take out the breading on the inside
  • 1lb of pork tenderloin, thinnly sliced - this will be marianted
  • Marinade - 2tbsp of soy sauce, 1tbsp of fish sauce, 1tbsp of sugar, 2 garlic cloves, 1 minced shallot, 1tsp of ground black pepper and 1tbsp of vegetable oil

Other Ingredients

  • Pickled vegetables
    • 1 cup of julienned carrots
    • 1/4 cup of rice vinegar
    • 1 cup of radish
    • 1/4 cup of water
    • 2tbsp of sugar
    • 1tsp of salt
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1tbsp of soy sauce
  • Fresh cilantro sprigs
  • Thinnly sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced jalapeños

Tips

  • Baguette Selection: Use a light, crispy baguette for authenticity. Avoid overly dense bread.
  • Custom Fillings: Swap pork for grilled chicken, tofu, pâté, or cold cuts if desired.
  • Serve: Enjoy immediately while the bread is crispy!

Directions

  1. Marinate the Pork: Combine soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, garlic, shallot, black pepper, and vegetable oil in a bowl. Add the pork slices and mix to coat evenly. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour or overnight for better flavor.
  2. Prepare the Pickled Vegetables: In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, water, sugar, and salt until dissolved. Add the julienned carrots and daikon radish to the mixture. Let sit for at least 30 minutes or up to overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Cook the Pork: Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated pork slices for 3-4 minutes on each side until fully cooked and slightly caramelized.
  4. Assemble the Bánh Mì: Slice the baguettes lengthwise, leaving one side attached. Lightly toast the baguettes if desired. Spread a layer of mayonnaise inside the baguette. For extra flavor, you can mix the mayo with a splash of soy sauce. Add a layer of cooked pork. Top with pickled vegetables, fresh cilantro sprigs, sliced cucumbers, and jalapeños for a spicy kick.
